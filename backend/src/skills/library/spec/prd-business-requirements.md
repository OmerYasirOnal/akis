Turn a business idea into a PRD that aligns stakeholders on what to build and why — not how. Produce:

1. Summary — the product/feature in 2-3 sentences and who it's for.
2. Problem statement — the user/business pain, current state, why now.
3. Goals & non-goals — measurable objectives, plus explicit out-of-scope items.
4. Users & personas — target segments and their jobs-to-be-done.
5. Requirements — prioritized (P0/P1/P2) functional requirements as "As a __, I want __, so that __", each with acceptance criteria.
6. User flows — the main happy path and key edge cases at a behavioral level.
7. Success metrics — KPIs and target values that define success.
8. Constraints, assumptions, risks & open questions.

Stay solution-agnostic — describe outcomes and behavior, not tech stack or APIs. Mark every unvalidated assumption explicitly.
